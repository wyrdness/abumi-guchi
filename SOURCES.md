## Cultural Sensitivity

> **Note**: Abumi-guchi folklore reflects samurai era Japanese culture, particularly the sacred bond between warrior and war horse. When studying this yokai, please recognize the cultural context of loyalty, sacrifice, and the spiritual significance placed on objects that witnessed tragedy.
